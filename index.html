More About Costa Rica


Costa Rica is divided into seven provinces which are San Jose (the capital), Alajuela, Cartago, Heredia, Puntarenas, Guanacaste, and Limon. With 81 cantons and 463 districts.

Official Language

Spanish with English being the second most spoken langauge.

Official Currency

Costa Rica Colon, $1 US dollar = 626.85 Colon.

Official Religion

Roman Catholic

Costa Rica covers on 0.03% of the surface of the globe.

Costa Rica shelters 5% of the exisiting biodiversity in the world.

One of Costa Rica's main source of income is the tourism the country attracts.
