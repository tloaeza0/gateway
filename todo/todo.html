Top Attractions

Wildlife Watching

You can experience wildlife like never before at Jaguar Centro De Rescate. They offer public or private tours where you can be in close proximity with monkeys, wild cats, sloths, antears and more. These terramiums offer animals a space where they can imitate their natural habitat.

Location: Punta Cocles, Costa Rica

Price: $22 USD = 13,816.01 Colones

Target Age: All ages!

Casacda verde

Also known as mother natures slide the Cascada Verde is a popular sight many people visit. This natural water slide has scuplted channel which makes the ride down bumpy but fun while also being safe. It has a natural swiiming pool which is actually swimmable and many ponds surroudning it which makes it safe for kids to get in.

Location: Uvita, Costa Rica

Price: 1.59 USD = 1000 Colones

Target Age: All Ages!

Venado Cave

Loacted in the northern lowlands of Costa Rica, Venado caves offers a glimpse into the under ground world of Costa Rica. Tours are offered where you can see rivers, fossils, caverns and strange subterranean creatures.

Location: San Carlos, Costa Rica

Price: 80 USD = 50,240.03 Colones

Target Age: All Ages!

Natural Science Museum La Salle

The museum of Natural Science Museum La Salle will give you a look into some of natures best creations ranging from its zoology and mineralogy. With nearly 55,000 different specimens from snakes, fish, turtles and more.

Location: San Jose, Costa Rica

Price: 1 USD = 628.24 Colones

Target Age: All ages!

Ecoglide Park

This park offers various tours that include fun activities such a ziplining. The Tarzan Swing reaches almost 40 meters high, swinging from a rope system jumping from a platform to the void of the forset.

Location: La Fortuna, Alajuela, Costa Rica

Price:31 USD = 19601.18 Colones

Target Age: Young Adults

Sunset Cruise

Enjoy a sunset cruise in Tamarindo. The cruise includes snorking, kayaking eqipment, open bar and lunch.

Location: Tamarindo, Costa Rica

Price:105 USD = 65965.49 Colones

Target Age: 21-50
